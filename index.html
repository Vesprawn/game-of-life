Game of Life

Rules

  • A neighbour is defined as the up to 8 cells surrounding a cell.
  • Any live cell survives if it has 2 or 3 neighbours.
  • Any dead cell with exactly 3 neighbours becomes a live cell.
  • All other cells die or remain dead.
  • Simulation ends when population stabalises or is wiped out.
  • There is a chance of occilasion where simulation will run forever.
